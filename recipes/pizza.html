Cheesy Pizza

Description

This is famous indian dish. It is made from cottage cheese and is very delisious to try and very easy to cook. If you want to give your taste buds a ride then make sure to try it out

Ingridients

  • Cottage Cheese/Paneer
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Green Chilli
  • Butter
  • Mil

Steps

  1. Wash the cheese/paneer under water
  2. Take a pan add 2 tbsp butter and onions to simmer
  3. Add Tomatoes when onion turns brown
  4. Add Milk
  5. Add Green Chilli
  6. Simmer for 5 minutes
  7. Enjoy
